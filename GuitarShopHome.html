Store

We know that you love to play. That is why we offer serious guitars for serious players. You know when the sound is right. You can feel it. You can hear it. That is why our selection encompasses iconic brands like Fender, Gibson, and Marshall.

Mission

Our mission is to help everyone from newbies to rock n roll hall of famers be better players. We believe that if someone wants to pick up an intstrument and start playing, they can do so in our store. We offer lessons for new players and ample space to play and test guitars and amps for all.

Lessons

Our instructors are well trained players and performance veterans who love to help people learn new intstruments or improve on their favorite. We believe in helping the student at their own pace and teaching them what they want to learn.
